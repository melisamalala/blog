This journal is simply a collection of all my thoughts and encounters in the world of code, design, business and projects I work on. It has code snippets of how I came to solve some challenges with code, design inspirations I use from time to time, also money problems and thoughts :D. Most are simple and others are bigger problems. Language focus is on Python, using the Flask and Django frameworks.

Frameworks I use a lot are Angular, Flask and Django as you will notice these are mainly in 3 categories:

    Mobile, which I consider a fundamental part to connecting to people at an individual level. I choose Android because of the large community support and also its very customizable API.
    Databases and Servers, we do need to store data and connect things together. Flask is highly customizable and allows one to architecture their product or idea the way they see fit. Django allows the fast development of the same architecture . Python is a very strong language and can do so much more than just backend interactions.
    User Interface , something that looks good will always attract. ReactJS is a very powerful JavaScript library that allows easy interaction with the DOM. Also it is very quick and easy to learn once you have the idea of seperation of concerns already locked-in to the way you do things.
